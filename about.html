What I'm About

I'm a User Experience Designer from British Columbia, Canada currently living in New Jersey, USA. Thanks to my study background in web design & development and user experience design, I know how to make beautiful products with user experiences that work even better than they look.

In my experience, websites should not only give information, but should communicate a unique experience that is specific to your brand.

Background Story

Following My Passion

I have always fought to find something that I would be excited to do each day. I was ecstatic when I first got my job as a UX Researcher. It was fascinating to see how wrong our assumptions can be and how much we let our own biases affect our work.

This foundational knowledge helped put everything into perspective once I became a UX Designer. I still conducted my own research for many of my projects, but on the ones I didn't, I understood the research better so that I knew how important it's application into the website was.

After doing this for a while, I still never get bored of going through the research, design, and development processes to improve a product. I am passionate about the effect that proper research and design can have on a website.
