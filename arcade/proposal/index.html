Final:Throwback Arcade Proposal

Purpose

The arcade will consist of 7 javascript-based games:

  • Memory Game
  • Connect Four
  • Space Invaders
  • Frogger
  • Whack-a-Mole
  • Snake
  • Tetris
It's purpose is to provide fun to individuals wanting to play games on their phone.

Audience

The audience for Leah's Javascript Arcade will be:

Age:

25-60 years old

Sex:

Male or Female

Location:

United States and Canada

Audience Likes:

  • Passing time while communting/waiting around
  • Reminiscing about old times
  • Playing games that they used to play that remind them of their childhood
  • Fun colors
  • Simple, non-complicated games

Audience Dislikes:

  • How fast everything has changed with technology
  • How complicated some new games can be
