Connect Four

Rules

Tap a space along the bottom row to get started. The aim for both players is to make a straight line of four own pieces; the line can be vertical, horizontal or diagonal. Pieces must be placed on the bottom row or on top of another piece in the same column.

The winner is the first player who gets a straight line made with four own pieces and no gaps between them.
